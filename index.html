Tegucigalpa Real Estate

The project focused on filling a data gap in the real estate domain of Tegucigalpa (Honduras) by scraping information from various real estate websites using Python scripts. AWS services, including Lambda and S3, facilitated the scheduling and automation of data extraction, transformation, and cleaning. Given the absence of pre-built datasets, this approach was vital in aggregating real estate data. The processed data was then visualized in a Power BI dashboard, offering a comprehensive view of trends and patterns. Hosted for accessibility, this dashboard serves as a valuable tool for understanding real estate dynamics through the integrated use of Python, AWS, and Power BI.

Beach Reservations

In this project, I used SQL to analyze beach reservation data through tables including Customer, Booking, Beach, Boat, Restaurant, and SpecialOffer. I explored top-rated beaches, customer preferences for boat rentals, and price trends. Tracking special offer utilization enabled me to derive actionable insights. The findings led to personalized offers, optimized revenue, and enhanced overall customer satisfaction, fulfilling various business goals.
